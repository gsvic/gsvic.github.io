About Me

I am a Computer Science PhD Student at Cornell, advised by Immanuel Trummer. My research interests lie in the area of database systems, mainly query optimization and database system tuning. During my PhD, I have interned at Microsoft Research and IBM Research working on similar topics. My work focuses on building query optimizers for federated query execution in distributed query engines, as well as leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs) for automated database system tuning. Prior to Cornell, I was a Senior Software Engineer at TileDB.
